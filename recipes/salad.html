Caesar Salad

The Classic Caesar Salad

A Caesar salad is a classic dish that brings together crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and a creamy, tangy dressing. The dressing, traditionally made with anchovies, garlic, lemon juice, egg yolks, and Parmesan cheese, imparts a rich umami flavor that distinguishes this salad from others. Often garnished with additional Parmesan and freshly ground black pepper, the Caesar salad offers a harmonious blend of textures and tastes, making it a staple in many households and restaurants.

Ingredients

For the Croutons:

  • 3 cups of day-old bread (such as sourdough or ciabatta), torn into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely grated
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the Dressing:

  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 4 anchovy fillets
  • 2 large egg yolks (preferably pasteurized)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 3/4 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons finely grated Parmesan cheese

For the Salad:

  • 2 medium romaine lettuce hearts, chopped or leaves separated
  • Additional grated Parmesan cheese, for garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare the Croutons:

    • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • In a small bowl, mix the grated garlic with olive oil.
    • Place the torn bread pieces on a baking sheet, drizzle with the garlic oil, and sprinkle with grated Parmesan. Toss to combine and spread into a single layer. Season lightly with salt and black pepper.
    • Bake until golden brown and crisp, about 10 to 15 minutes.

  2. Make the Dressing

    • In a blender, combine lemon juice, anchovy fillets, egg yolks, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, and black pepper. Blend on high speed until smooth, about 15 seconds.
    • With the blender running, slowly stream in the olive oil until the dressing is thick and creamy.
    • Transfer the dressing to a bowl and stir in the finely grated Parmesan cheese.

  3. Assemble the Salad

    • In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce with the dressing and half of the croutons. Toss to evenly coat.
    • Garnish with the remaining croutons and additional grated Parmesan cheese.
    • Serve immediately.
