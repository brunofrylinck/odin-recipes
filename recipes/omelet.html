Delicious and Simple Omelet Recipe

This simple omelet recipe is perfect for a quick, delicious meal. It's light, fluffy, and totally customizable. With just eggs, salt, pepper, and butter, ou can make a tasty base, and then add whatever fillings you like—cheese, veggies, or even ham. The eggs cook up soft and creamy, and when folded, you get a warm, comforting bite. It’s an easy, go-to dish that works for breakfast, lunch, or dinner!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon butteer or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, mushroooms, onions, bell peppers, spinach, ham, etc.

Steps

  1. Prepare the eggs: Crack the egs into a bowl and whisk with a pinch of salt and pepper until fully combined.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick skillet or frying pan on medium heat. Add the butter or oil and let it melt, swirling it around to coat the bottom of the pan.
  3. Cook the eggs: Pour the beaten eggs into the pan. Let them cook undisturbed for about 1-2 minutes until the edges start to set.
  4. Add fillings (optional): If you're adding fillings like cheese or veggies, sprinkle them over half of the omelet now.
  5. Fold the omelet: Once the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny on top, use a spatula to gently fold the omelet in half.
  6. Finish cooking: Let the omelet cook for another 30 seconds to 1 minute, or until fully set but still soft inside.
  7. Serve: Slide the omelet onto a plate and enjoy!
